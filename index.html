"Build-A-PC" | Community Org Workshop

Designed for Free Geek Twin Cities, this one-day workshop curriculum empowers community members to become environmental stewards by learning how computers are built and maintained with a priority on refurbished equipment. Through hands-on instruction, learners identify essential hardware components, assemble a fully functional PC to take with them, and install an operating system. The course concludes by equipping participants with critical technical life skills, including electrical safety, navigating component compatibility, and ensuring proper data destruction during electronics recycling.
